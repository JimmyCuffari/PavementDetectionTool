Pavement Dataset Tool

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

What we collect

This tool requests access to your Google account in order to:

  • Identify you by email address (for activity tracking in the shared dataset log)
  • Read and write files in Google Drive that this app creates

How we use it

Your email and the actions you take (frame extraction, label upload) are recorded in a shared tracking.json file stored in your team's Google Drive. No data is sent to any external server — all processing happens in your browser.

Data retention

Data lives in your team's Google Drive and is controlled by your Google account. You can revoke app access at any time via Google Account Permissions.

Contact

For questions, contact your project administrator.
